### Cutting-Edge Research

1.  **The LLM-in-Sandbox paradigm has officially dropped!** A [joint research effort by Renmin University, Microsoft, and Tsinghua (AI News)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16206) now lets models freely explore within a sandbox environment. This setup has led to significant improvements across multiple domains, including math, physics, and chemistry ‚Äì super impressive! It also slashes long text token consumption by 8 times and works without any extra training. A Python package is already open source, supporting mainstream inference backends. Pretty cool, right?
2.  **SenseTime has open-sourced its MARS multimodal model!** [SenseNova-MARS (AI News)](https://www.jiqizhixin.com/articles/2026-01-30) has crushed it, scoring 69.74 and surpassing Gemini-3-Pro. This bad boy is the first agentic VLM to support dynamic visual reasoning, autonomously calling image cropping and text search tools. Both 8B and 32B versions are open source, and the technical report is already up on arXiv. What a win!
3.  **New AI coding efficiency research is out from Anthropic!** A [randomized controlled trial (AI News)](https://newshacker.me/story?id=46821360) published by Anthropic shows that AI assistance doesn't necessarily speed things up for everyone. Beginners' conceptual understanding and debugging abilities can actually take a hit. Interestingly, seasoned developers seem to benefit more. The study used the GPT-4o model, and its implications at an organizational level are worth paying attention to. Something to think about!
